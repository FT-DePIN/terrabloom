Quick Answers

When will my box arrive?

Deliveries are made Tuesday–Saturday. You'll receive an email with a 2-hour arrival window the morning of your delivery day.

Can I skip a week?

Absolutely! Log in to your account and skip up to 4 weeks in a row, no fees. Just make sure to do it before 11:59 PM on Sunday.

Do you deliver to my area?

We currently serve the Pacific Northwest, California, Texas, and the Midwest. Enter your ZIP code at checkout to confirm availability.
