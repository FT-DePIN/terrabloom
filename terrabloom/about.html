Our Beginnings

From a Backyard Garden to 12 Partner Farms

TerraBloom Organics started in the summer of 2020, when co-founders Elena Marsh and David Okafor returned to their family farms after years in the corporate world. Alarmed by the growing gap between consumers and the food they ate, they made a pact: to rebuild that connection.

What began as a small neighborhood delivery pilot with three farms and 40 subscribers quickly grew beyond anything they imagined. By 2021, TerraBloom had expanded to serve the entire Pacific Northwest. By 2023, they welcomed partners across Texas, the Midwest, and the Northeast.

Today, TerraBloom works with 12 certified organic farms, delivers to over 4,200 households weekly, and maintains a 98% customer satisfaction rating — a number we wear with immense pride and responsibility.

Our Journey

Milestones That Made Us

From a garage pilot to a national movement — here are the moments that shaped TerraBloom.

SUMMER 2020

The Seed Is Planted

Elena and David launch a local pilot with 40 subscribers and 3 farms in Eugene, Oregon.

MARCH 2021

Pacific Northwest Launch

TerraBloom expands to serve all of Oregon and Washington with 500 subscribers.

JANUARY 2022

USDA Organic Certification

All 8 partner farms achieve USDA Organic Certification. Dr. Maya Patel joins as Head of Nutrition.

JULY 2023

National Expansion

TerraBloom launches in Texas and the Midwest, surpassing 2,500 active subscribers.

2024

Herb Collection Launch

We introduce the Gourmet Herb & Spice Collection and hit 4,000 subscribers — and a 98% satisfaction score.

2025–PRESENT

Growing Strong

Featured in Organic Life Magazine's "Top 10 Food Tech Companies." Now serving 4,200+ households nationwide.
